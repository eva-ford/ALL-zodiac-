Aquarius

An intellectual, deep, and social individual who cares deeply about other, their community, and mankind. Always focused on the greater good, they have an optimistic take on life and its challenges. Ruled more by the head than the heart, they reach for the stars when it comes to their personal endeavours. You can always count on Aquarius for great ideas and fun conversations, and they are always willing to lend a hand or an ear. In love and friendship, Aquarius is friendly, but it keeps others at a distance due to its detached nature. Friends and lovers can feel frustrated by their inability to build intimacy in their relationships. When Aquarius continues his quest for knowledge, he comes up with ideas ranging from brilliant to insane. Distinguish between the two and you're more likely to be accepted as an expert.

  • Strengths: Progressive, original, independent, humanitarian
  • Weaknesses: Runs from emotional expression, temperamental, uncompromising
  • Aquarius likes: Fun with friends, fighting for causes, intellectual conversations, working hard
  • Aquarius disikes: Limitations, broken promises, being lonely, dull situations.

Pisces

Pisces signs are sensitive, and get along well in small groups of people. Sometimes, a Pisces may feel like they have an internal and external self, and they may need to spend a lot of time solo to recalibrate those two halves of themselves. A Pisces is rarely lonely when they are by themselves, and have an active imagination. Creative, Pisces love spending time reading, exploring or creating art or music, and understanding their emotions through art. Deeply intelligent, Pisces have a profound respect for the power of the human mind and often get lost in their thoughts.

  • Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle, wise, musical
  • Weaknesses: Fearful, overly trusting, sad, desire to escape reality
  • Pisces likes: Being alone, love, sleeping, music, romance, swimming, spiritual themes
  • Pisces disikes: Know-it-all, being criticized, the past coming back to haunt, cruelty of any kind.

Aries

Aries are naturally brave and focused individuals that lead with their head rather than the heart. They possess youthful strength and energy, regardless of their age and quickly perform any given tasks. They thrive in competition and love being the first in everything. Always having an idea of what they want, they do great under a leadership role. Driven by the desire to improve their strengths, they have high expectations. They also have a love for adventure and a sense to explore.

  • Strengths: Courageous, determined, confident, enthusiastic, optimistic, honest, passionate
  • Weaknesses: Impatient, moody, short-tempered, impulsive, aggressive
  • Aries likes: Comfortable clothes, taking on leadership roles, physical challenges, individual sports
  • Aries disikes: Inactivity, delays, work that does not use one's talents

Taurus

Taurus always see things from a grounded, realistic and practical perspective. They find that money comes to them when they dont go out of their way to find it. Taurus is often found to be amazing cooks, gardener, and lover. They are highly loyal signs and dont enjoy making others upset. However, they dont like sudden change and often attach themselves to others rather quickly. As an Earch sign, they can be overprotective of their loved ones, conservative, and materialistic..

  • Strengths:Reliable, patient, practical, devoted, responsible, stable
  • Weaknesses: Stubborn, possessive, uncompromising
  • Taurus likes: Gardening, cooking, music, romance, high quality clothes, working with hands
  • Taurus disikes: Sudden changes, complications, insecurity of any kind, synthetic fabrics

Gemini

Gemini are excellent artists, especially singers and writers due to them seeing love through verbal contact. Those born under this sign are flexible, inquisitive and fun-loving, with a strong desire to experience everything in the world. They look for ever new experiences, making their character inspiring, and never boring.It is imperative for them to find excitement, variety, and passion, and when they find the right person, a friend, a lover and someone to share a moment with, they will remain faithful and devoted to never lose their hearts. Despite their unfair reputation for being two-faced, once a Gemini is in your life, they're loyal for life-but they won't refrain from being opinionated if they know you're acting in a non-loyal way or if they see you acting in an untrustworthy manner.

  • Strengths: Gentle, affectionate, curious, adaptable, ability to learn quickly and exchange ideas
  • Weaknesses:Nervous, inconsistent, indecisive
  • Gemini likes: Music, books, magazines, chats with nearly anyone, short trips around the town
  • Gemini disikes: Being alone, being confined, repetition and routine

Cancer

Cancers are known to be the most psychic signs with high levels of intuit ideas, and advice. Social gatherings, especially with people the sign is not close to can be overwhelming for Cancers. They do their best with a small group of same-minded individuals. Rarely benefiting from close combat of anykinf, they are quick to avoid conflict, just as quick as they are to help others especially in times of need. Cancer signs may seem prickly and standoffish at first meeting, once they make the decision to become friends with someone, that person has a friend for life. Cancers thrive off harmony, and get a good look for creating strong intimate relationships with peers, significant others and family members. Cancers also love creating and find the need to have some sort of creative outlet, whether its painting, writing, dancing, or even just reading.
In romance, Cancer is a giving and hopeful lover who expects the same in return. They like to be blunt with their lover, and hates the “chasing game”. As they are deeply intimate, it is not uncommon for Cancers to fall in love after just a few days or weeks. A sudden decision for them can last a lifetime!

  • Strengths: Tenacious, highly imaginative, loyal, emotional, sympathetic, persuasive
  • Weaknesses: Moody, pessimistic, suspicious, manipulative, insecure
  • Cancer likes: Art, home-based hobbies, relaxing near or in water, helping loved ones, a good meal with friends
  • Cancer disikes: Strangers, any criticism of Mom, revealing of personal life.

Leo

Represented as a symbol to lions, Leos are natural born leaders, dramatic, dominant, and self-confident. They are able to achieve anything they want in life, all they have to do is commit to the project. Dont let their dominance fool you, as they are warmhearted, in love with life, love to laugh and simply enjoy the little things in their life. Very confident individuals, they love themselves and would do anything to grow their ego, and are extremely self-aware. Their high standards come in good use when they interact with others. Always with their head high, they speak to others in respect and high standard. But when a Leo becomes too attached to their own achievements, others could turn away.

  • Strengths: Creative, passionate, generous, warm-hearted, cheerful, humorous
  • Weaknesses: Arrogant, stubborn, self-centered, lazy, inflexible
  • Leos likes: Creativity, vacations, being admired, expensive things, bright colors, time with friends
  • Leos disikes: Being ignored, facing difficult reality, not being treated like a king or queen.

Virgo

A strong character, Virgo, are well-organzied, conservative, goal-orientated individuals. Keeping their goals at the front-end of their mind, they wont let chaos get in the way. They can become obsessed with details, constantly worrying that they missed something important that they cannot fix, and may become overly critical and concerned about matters that no one else seems to care about. Never wanting to disappoint another person, they get easily offended when the smallest inconvience happens, like a last-minute cancellation. Virgos often get lost in the business of life, at times of difficulty, Virgos find themselves separating from loved ones, criticizing, and judging. Virgos are inspired by beauty, by outfits, nature, organization. They thrive when everything in their life is neat and “picture perfect”.

  • Strengths: Loyal, analytical, kind, hardworking, practical
  • Weaknesses: Shy, overthinker, overly critical of self and others, all work and no play
  • Virgo likes: Animals, healthy food, books, nature, cleanliness
  • Virgo disikes: Rude people, asking for help, center of attention

Libra

Libras are peaceful, fair, and love the company of others. Being an Air sign, along with Gemini and Aquarius, they get inspired by good works of literature, have a strong intellect, and have a keen mind. Libras live off of balance and symmetry, and justice and equality. They take the big-picture approach when trying to make their life the best that they can be. Libras get fixated on one thing, whether it's work, a partner, or a family member, and this can leave them unhappy and needing change. Although Libra appears self-confident to outsiders, they might struggle with insecurity and low confidence, especially as it relates to personal identity, which sometimes feels mutable. Love being around people, Libras also need time on their own, to understand who they are. When Libras fall in love, they fall hard, but when it is time to go, it's easy for Libra to detach themselves from the situation.

  • Strengths: Cooperative, diplomatic, gracious, fair-minded, social
  • Weaknesses: Indecisive, avoids confrontations, will carry a grudge, self-pity
  • Libras likes: Balance, gentleness, sharing with others, nature
  • Libras disikes: Violence, injustice, loudmouths, conformity

Scorpio

Scorpios show high levels of determination and once they find someone or something that inspires them, they become very passionate and dominant. Being impressive individuals when a challenge arises, Scorpios are fearless and resourceful. Even though Scorpios give off cool for you vibes, they are also known to be very calm and cool, looking mysterious and hard to figure out. A Scorpio has a deep understanding of the rules of the Universe, which gives them an advantage others don't realize exists. Scorpios are Water signs, just as Cancer and Pisces, and Water signs are said that they always fight dishonesty of any kind, so they have to learn how to manipulate others to understand their behavior and thoughts. These characteristics lead them to acquire strange behavior patterns that are related to their inner tendencies such as distrust and jealousy.

  • Strengths:Resourceful, powerful, brave, passionate, a true friend
  • Weaknesses: Distrusting, jealous, manipulative, violent
  • Scorpio likes: Truth, facts, being right, talents, teasing, passion
  • Scorpio disikes: Dishonesty, revealing secrets, superficiality, small talk
Fun Fact!
Scorpios are passionate to the core.

Sagittarius

Sagittarius is an independent, adventurous, and strong-willed person. They often go away from the pack to explore the horizons by themselves. As natural-born leaders, they do what they want, regardless of what other people think. They have a deep understanding of one's self and loves to challenge their inner mind. In search of meaning in life, Sagittarius is the traveler of the Zodiac. They like to wander around the world, motivated by their open minds and philosophical views. Always optimistic, Sagittarius are extroverts, and love being in large circles. A fire sign, just like Aries and Leo, always has clear intentions and wants to shed its colors all around the world. Sagittarius is an extrovert, always optimistic, full of enthusiasm, and ready for changes. Sun signs tend to be mentally focused, but when they find grounding, they become able to translate their thoughts and visions into actions and circumstances that produce a tangible result.

  • Strengths: Generous, idealistic, great sense of humor
  • Weaknesses: Promises more than can deliver, very impatient, will say anything no matter how undiplomatic
  • Sagittarius likes: Freedom, travel, philosophy, being outdoors
  • Sagittarius disikes: Clingy people, being constrained, off-the-wall theories, details

Capricorn

The best zodiac sign. Doesnt have to do with the fact that it is my zodiac sign. It's just scientifically proven..... Anyways, extremely smart, hardworking individuals, Capricorn signs will always get what they set their mind to, in both personal and professional life. Capricorns love to succeed in all areas of life. They're great at climbing the corporate ladder, and can easily adapt to new roles. Capricorns believe presentation is everything, and their homes are usually a real-life Pinterest board. A Capricorn sometimes is overly focused on what things look like, instead of how things feel, which could cause them to feel unsatisfied. Capricorns love their family and are keen on good traditions. They are very serious by nature and possess an inner state of independence that controls their personal and professional lives. They possess self-control and can control a large group of people that work for them at any given time, make plans that are both solid and realistic, and lead others with their leadership. They will learn from their mistakes and get to the top solely on their experience and expertise.

  • Strengths: Responsible, disciplined, self-control, good managers
  • Weaknesses:Know-it-all, unforgiving, condescending, expecting the worst
  • Capricorn likes: Family, tradition, music, understated status, quality craftsmanship
  • Capricorn disikes: Almost everything at some point
